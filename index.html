About Me

LLH is a German artist and producer whose music lives between genres, shifting from rap and electronic to cinematic soundscapes. What started with piano lessons and choir solos evolved into self-produced projects that explore both storytelling and raw emotion. With influences ranging from Kanye West to Grimes and even Rammstein, LLH blends worlds that usually don’t meet, creating something unique, personal, and unpredictable.
