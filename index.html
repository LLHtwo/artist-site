About Me

LLH is a 20-year-old artist from Germany whose music refuses to sit in one genre. Across rap, house, techno, and experimental electronic, his projects capture different sides of his creativity — sometimes raw and spontaneous, other times ambitious and carefully shaped. What began as just a way to process thoughts and moods has grown into a series of albums that each stand in their own space. From the loose, unfiltered spirit of his early work to the more conceptual direction of recent releases like Spacewalk and Born For More, LLH’s catalog shows constant change and a refusal to repeat himself. At the center of it all is honesty, whether in a hard-hitting rap track or a drifting electronic piece, the music stays personal and true to where he’s at in life. And he’s nowhere near finished.
